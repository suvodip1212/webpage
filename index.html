Advancing Cognition-Aware Systems

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at ETH Zurich, affiliated with the Future Resilient Systems (FRS) programme at the Singapore-ETH Centre. My expertise lies at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Applied Eye-Tracking.

My work aims to create interfaces that sense a user's mental state via eye-tracking (such as Benign Anisocoria metrics) to support critical decision-making in urban and industrial systems.
